Villa Mondragone, Frascati, June 14-16, 2011

Call for contributions

Authors wishing to submit a contribution, should prepare an extended abstract of 3-4 pages by March 14th, 2011.

Please note that each participant can present at most one paper.

To submit a contribution, please prepare an extended abstract of at most 4 pages and submit through the EasyChair conference system no later than Monday, March 14th, 2011. Please use the following template, which is based on the LaTeX class "ctwart".

Upload a PostScript or PDF version of your abstract using the EasyChair submission page at

https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=ctw2011

If you do not have an EasyChair account you will be asked to create one. This is mandatory in order to submit your contribution to CTW 2011.

The scientific committee will select the presentations for the workshop on the basis of the extended abstracts received. If your contribution is accepted, you will later be asked to submit the LaTeX source, too.

 

Submission server is now closed.
