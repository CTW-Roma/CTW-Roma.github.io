Submission server is closed

 

Submission of revised contribution

The final version of the extended abstract to be included in the conference proceedings  should be submitted  by May 6th, 2011 through EasyChair

https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=ctw2011

 

Please note that the final extendended abstract:

  • should be prepared using the following template, which is based on the LaTeX class "ctwart";
  • must be of at most 4 pages;
  • must be revised according to reviewer's suggestions, if any.

 

Moreover, authors are required to upload on easychair both the LaTex source file and the PDF output.

Special Issue

A special issue of Discrete Applied Mathematics, containing full length papers, refereed according to the high standards of DAM, is planned and will be advertised after the conference.
